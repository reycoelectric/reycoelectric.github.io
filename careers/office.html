Open Roles

What We're Building

Role

Project Manager

~8-year average PM tenure. Strong project pipeline. Real autonomy.

You own the full lifecycle of assigned projects from award through close-out. You run weekly project meetings with the superintendent and foremen, track cost-to-complete, manage submittals and scheduling, and coordinate with GCs and owners.

What you'll use

BuildOps for project tracking and job costing. AccuBid data for buyout. QuickBooks for financial reporting.

What makes it different

Estimators stay involved through buyout — you don't inherit blind spots. The superintendent is your partner. Dave is accessible when you need a decision.

Role

Estimator

Selective bid volume. Relationship-driven work. Margins that hold.

You put numbers together on plan-spec commercial electrical work. You use AccuBid for takeoffs, manage the bid log, and coordinate with the PM team on buyout after award. At Reyco, estimators don't just throw numbers over the wall — you stay involved through buyout to make sure what you estimated is what gets built.

Bid volume

We target negotiated and relationship-driven work over hard-bid commodity jobs. You won't be cranking out 50 bids a month for work we'll never win.

What you'll use

AccuBid for estimating. BuildOps integration coming. Plan sets, submittals, and vendor coordination.

Role

Account Manager

68+ active accounts. DMV territory. Expanding to Richmond and NoVA.

You own a book of business across QSR, multifamily, and institutional accounts. You visit facilities, identify service needs, put together proposals for work that doesn't come with drawings, and make sure Reyco is the first call when something goes wrong. You're measured on bids out, dollars returned, new customer development, and service-to-project conversion.

The territory

The DMV. Restaurants, apartment buildings, institutional facilities, and retail. Reyco is actively expanding into Northern Virginia and Richmond.

What makes it different

No single person "owns" a customer — it's Reyco's relationship. Multiple people touch every account, so you have support and coverage.

Also Hiring

Operations & Administrative Roles

FP&A, Service Coordination, and warehouse operations. If you want to work at a company that's growing, uses real systems, and has a back-office team with ~10-year average tenure, reach out.
