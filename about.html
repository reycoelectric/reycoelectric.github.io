The Founding Story

Dave Reynolds, Founder & President

Dave Reynolds founded Reyco Electrical Services in 2005 with one truck, one phone, and a commitment to showing up when he said he would. Twenty-one years later, Reyco is a 53-person operation serving the most demanding commercial and institutional facilities in DC, Maryland, and Virginia.

From emergency service calls at 2 AM to multi-year institutional projects, Reyco's team handles the electrical work that keeps mission-critical facilities running. Our safety record — EMR 0.81, top 10% nationally — means we're trusted in environments where other contractors can't even get through the door.

Dave still runs the company day-to-day. He knows every tech by name, is personally involved in hiring, and is focused on building a company where people can grow their careers — not just collect a paycheck.

How We Operate

Safety First

How We Work

Modern Systems, Real Accountability

We use BuildOps to dispatch, track, and invoice every service call. Our techs carry tablets with real-time job details, customer information, and the ability to capture photos, collect signatures, and generate invoices on-site.

That means you get a digital invoice the same day we're on-site — not 45 days later. And our account managers can see the status of every call in real time, so there's no phone tag and no surprises.

Larger projects are estimated through AccuBid and all costs sync to QuickBooks. We run tight, documented processes — our folder structures, job workflows, and dispatch systems have been refined over 10+ years.
