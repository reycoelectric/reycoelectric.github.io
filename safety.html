Our Safety Record

EMR 0.81 — Top 10% Nationally

EMR stands for Experience Modification Rate — the insurance industry's measure of a contractor's safety performance relative to their peers. An EMR of 1.0 is average. Below 1.0 means you're safer than average. Our EMR of 0.81 places Reyco in the top 10% of electrical contractors in the country.

That number matters because many institutional and government facilities — the World Bank, IFC, IDB, and others — require a minimum EMR to even bid on work. Average-safety contractors can't get through the door. We can.

Dave doesn't talk about this in meetings unprompted. It's just how we operate. The number reflects what happens when you hire carefully, train consistently, and take safety seriously every single day.
